## üìñ Overview
This interactive web application enables users to visualize the impact of **Compound Interest** on their savings over time. 

Specifically designed for the Mexican market context, it compares high-yield investment strategies (such as **CETES** or **SOFIPOS** with 10-15% APY) against traditional cash savings (0% yield), mathematically demonstrating the time-value of money.

## üöÄ Key Features
* **Financial Projection:** Real-time calculation of monthly and annual returns.
* **Scenario Simulation:** Interactive sliders to adjust parameters:
    * Initial Capital ($ MXN).
    * Monthly Contributions.
    * Annual Interest Rate (%).
    * Time Horizon (Years).
* **Data Visualization:** Dynamic line charts comparing "Investment" vs "Cash" growth curves.
* **KPI Analysis:** Key metrics highlighting Net Profit generated.
